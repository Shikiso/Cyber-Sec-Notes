# Acceptable Use Policy
- Most common
- What users can and cannot do with regard to organizational computing assets such as computers, data, network connections and internet.
# Data Sensitivity and Classification Policy
- States what the levels are, what protections are required for them an4d under what circumstances these sensitivity labels must be used.
# Access Control Policy
- Organizations requirements for accessing systems and data
- May state the different data sensitivity levels and describe access requirements for each
# Password Policy
- Requirements for strong passwords
- If password authentication is used
- Restrictions on password sharing
# Care and Use of Equipment
- Keep equipment clean and serviceable
- Periodic maintenance requirements and restrictions on employees removing equipment from company premises
# Privacy Policy
- Applies to everyone, customers and employees
- Lack of expectation of privacy when using company computers
- Right of the organization to monitor and view personal data
# Personnel Policies
- How employees interact with systems and data
- How organization manages personnel from a security perspective
