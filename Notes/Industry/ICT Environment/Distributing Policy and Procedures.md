**Policy Distribution**
The process of sharing new or updated policies with all required stakeholders.

**Stakeholder Communication**
The process of sharing timely, clear and relevant information with people involved with the organisation.

**Organisational Procedures**
The approved methods and steps staff must follow when distributing official documents.

# Why Policy Distribution is Important
**Distribution of policies helps to:**
- Informs stakeholders of change
- Helps them understand the changes
- Supports compliance and consitency
- Reduces operational risk from awareness
- Promotes transparent communication
# Understanding Policy Updates
**Helps to:**
- Identify what has changed
- Clarify the purpose of updates
- Review impacts on teams
- Prepare information for distribution
# Identify Key Stakeholders
**Stakeholders may include:**
- Internal staff
- Managers
- Team leaders
- External partners
- Contractors and service providers
# Stakeholder Needs
**Stakeholders should be provided with:**
- Clear explanation of content
- Relevant operational chargers
- Updated Responsibilities
- Available support resources
# Organisational Distribution Requirements
**Distribution should:**
- Use approved communication methods
- Follow internal communication protocols
- Maintain version-control standards
- Document distribution activities
# Approved Communication Channels
- Email announcements
- Intranet updates
- Staff meetings
- Noticeboards
- Online document portals
# Distributing the new/updated policies
**New or updated policies should:**
- Provide current policy version
- Attach a summary of changes
- Include implementation dates
- Share contact information for further clarification or help with understanding the new/updated policies
# Stakeholder understanding
**Check stakeholders understanding by:**
- Providing clarification opportunities
- Encouraging questions and feedback
- Offering additional training
- Providing support materials
# Confirming understanding
**Can be done by:**
- Requesting read-confirmations
- Track inbox delivery if required
- Log stakeholder acknowledgement
- Verbal or online follow ups with staff
# Record-keeping requirements
**It is important to:**
- Maintain distribution records
- Store communication logs
- File updated policy versions
- Ensure compliance with standards
# Best Practices
- Communicate clearly and early
- Use plain language
- Be consistent and timely
- Make documents easy to access
